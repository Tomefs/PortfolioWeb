My goal is to lend you a hand and help you bring your ideas and visions to life through creating professional and meaningfull identities and graphics.

Hello there! I’m Tomé Simões. I’m a graphic designer from Portugal, currently studying Design & Multimedia at the University of Coimbra. I studied Visual Arts in high school, at the time I had no idea what design was, I just enjoyed drawing and I was good at it. I started creating and editing videos for fun. Later I had my first job for a futsal club where I was asked to create some designs for posters and videos.

Right now I’m starting my own path in the world of design and multimedia. My main focus is based on creating visual identities and graphic designs for print and social media such as posters, flyers, business cards, animations, social media posts, web design, illustrations and more.
